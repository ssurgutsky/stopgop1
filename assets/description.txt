Verbal-Interactive video quest.
You went by your business and met the Gop. He wants to take your money and mobile. Answering his questions you have to get rid of him or even better if you take the money from himself.
In total 10 possible finals (1 main, 2 neutral and 7 negative)

This game is a text video quest game. It's not a monotonous thing. The main feature is in dialogs (questions-answers) between a player and main hero. Player must answer main hero questions to avoid problems and even take over of the main hero.
